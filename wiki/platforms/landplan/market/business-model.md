# LandPlan Business Model

---

## Pricing Strategy

Consumer SaaS. Target: $15–30/mo per property owner (1 owner + family/stakeholders per plan).

| Anchor | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|
| Land id® Pro at $50/mo | Nearest audience overlap (individual landowners). LandPlan undercuts meaningfully while delivering a dramatically richer planning capability. |
| onX Hunt at $30–100/yr | Demonstrates consumer willingness to pay for mobile land tools. |
| Contractor tools at $199–$499/mo | Not the target — but validates that land-adjacent SaaS can monetise well. |

**Estimated entry tier:** ~$15–30/mo. Free trial TBD.

---

## Revenue Streams (Planned)

1. **Web subscription** — monthly/annual access to [[landplan-app]]
2. **Hardware margin** — [[survey-gps-receiver]] device sale
3. **Potential B2B / contractor tier** — contractors who want persistent accounts (not explored yet)

---

## Key Differentiators that Justify Pricing

- Long-horizon project planning (unique in the consumer land segment)
- Contractor quote packages (unique)
- Data portability via export (trust differentiator)
- 3D visualisation + shadow simulation (unique at this price point)

---

## Competitive Features Competitors Have that LandPlan Should Prioritise

*(Drawn from Land id® benchmarking)*

- Nationwide parcel data and ownership details for 150M+ properties
- Rich GIS layers: soil, floodplains, contours, public lands, ownership boundaries
- Mobile app (iOS and Android) with offline access
- Map sharing by embed, link, or email
- Automated time and material estimating from contractor pricing databases
- Visual proposals with colour-coded project sitemaps
- Digital plan and blueprint uploads with measurement layering
- Material quantity calculations and cost estimates
