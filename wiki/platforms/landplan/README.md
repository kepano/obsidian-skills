# LandPlan Platform

> [!abstract] Overview
> LandPlan is a suite of three interconnected GIS and land-management products built for landowners. All three share the same spatial data domain and are designed to work together across web, mobile, and hardware form factors.
>
> **Tagline:** *"LandPlan — Visualize the future of your property."*

---

## The Three Products

### 1. LandPlan.app
[[products/landplan-app|LandPlan.app]]

A website and GIS system for landowners to visualise the future of their property. Combines interactive GIS mapping with long-horizon project planning, 3D models, shadow simulation, stakeholder collaboration, and contractor quoting.

### 2. LandPlan Survey (Mobile App)
[[products/survey-mobile|LandPlan Survey]]

A native iOS and Android mobile app for collecting GPS measurements in the field and syncing them to LandPlan.app. Enables ground-truth data capture without a computer. Connect to the GPS Receiver for survey-grade accuracy.

### 3. LandPlan Survey GPS Receiver (Hardware)
[[products/survey-gps-receiver|LandPlan Survey GPS Receiver]]

A physical device that connects to a smartphone via Bluetooth and a high-accuracy RTK GPS antenna via USB, delivering centimetre-level coordinates to the mobile app.
