# LandPlan Platform

> [!abstract] Overview
> LandPlan is a suite of three interconnected GIS and land-management products built for landowners, foresters, and land surveyors. All three products share the same spatial data domain and are designed to work together across web, mobile, and hardware form factors.

---

## The Three Products

Product names are not yet finalised. Placeholder wikilinks will be updated once names are confirmed.

### Product 1 — Web GIS Platform
[[landplan-web]] *(name TBD)*

A website and GIS system for landowners to visualise the future of their property. Combines interactive GIS mapping with project planning tools, allowing users to annotate their land with structures, water features, hazards, vegetation, and custom markers.

### Product 2 — Mobile Survey App
[[landplan-survey]] *(name TBD)*

A native iOS and Android mobile app for collecting GPS measurements in the field and syncing them back to the web platform. Enables ground-truth data capture without a computer.

### Product 3 — GPS Hardware Device
[[landplan-gps-pro]] *(name TBD)*

A physical device that connects to a smartphone via Bluetooth, a high-accuracy GPS receiver via USB, and a 360° camera — providing a low-cost alternative to professional survey equipment and Google Street View–style capture.
