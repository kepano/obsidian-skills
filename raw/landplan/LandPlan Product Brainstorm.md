
## [[LandPlan.app]]
A website and GIS system for LandOwners to visualize the future of their property. Combines GIS and Project planning.
## [[LandPlan Survey]]
A mobile app for iOS and Android that runs natively to collect GPS measurements of the real world and synch them with LandPlan.app.
## [[LandPlan GPS Pro]]
A physical device that connects to your phone via Bluetooth while connecting to a GPS receiver with antenna over USB, and connecting to a 360 cam to provide a poor man’s Google Street View.
